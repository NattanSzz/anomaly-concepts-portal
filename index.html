About

Anomaly Concepts is a narrative survival horror game where concepts take physical form — and need to be exterminated. Developed in Brazil, the game combines interactive storytelling, exploration, and strategic combat in a hybrid experience that blends psychological tension with dynamic action. Every choice matters. Every confrontation has consequences.

Features

Dialogue-Based

A narrative driven by intense dialogue and moral choices. Your decisions shape destiny — and not all truth needs to be discovered. Two paths. Two outcomes. Neither without cost.

Free Movement

Free and investigative exploration. Navigate through dark environments with complete control. Investigate scenarios, discover hidden clues, and prepare for the unexpected—because not every anomaly reveals itself immediately.

Quick Time Event

Moments of Extreme Reaction. In decisive confrontations, your speed can be the difference between survival and death. Quick reflexes will be required when tensions reach their peak.

Scarce Resources

Real scarcity, real decisions. Limited ammunition. Restricted resources. Every choice matters—attacking without thinking could cost you your survival.

Buy and Upgrade Weapons

Customizable Arsenal. Acquire and upgrade weapons to face increasingly lethal concepts. Adapt your strategy, strengthen your equipment, and prepare for unpredictable confrontations.

Story

A War Against Ideas Taken on Flesh.
Battista Nato, a supposed priest and former police officer, works for a secret organization dedicated to eliminating anomalies—physical manifestations of distorted human concepts.
However, there's always something behind the scenes...
This is not a story about saving the world.
The organization is not a league of heroes.
And Battista is not Justice.

Creators

Natanael Souza

Developer & Writer
The developer and writer behind Anomaly Concepts, responsible for the game's programming, narrative development, and overall design.
Combining storytelling with gameplay systems, he leads the creation of the game's world, mechanics, and evolving narrative.

Kevin Souza

Designer & Concept Collaborator
Kevin Souza contributes to the visual development of Anomaly Concepts, creating the game's artwork and helping shape the original concept during early development.
His artistic work brings the characters, anomalies, and atmosphere of the game to life.
